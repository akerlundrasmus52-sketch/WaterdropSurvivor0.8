⚠️ Early Alpha Notice

This game is in early alpha development. Sounds and music are pending and will be added in future updates.

🎮 Controls

  • Left Stick: Move your water drop character
  • Right Stick: Manual aim (optional) - aim where you want to shoot
  • Auto-Aim: When right stick is idle, auto-aim targets nearest enemy
  • Toggle Auto-Aim: Use the settings button to turn auto-aim on/off

📱 Recommended: Portrait Mode

For the best mobile experience, play in portrait orientation. Landscape mode is supported but portrait offers better visibility and control.

🎯 Game Mechanics

  • Camera: Top-down view follows your character
  • Quests: Complete objectives to earn rewards
  • Combos: Chain kills quickly for bonus XP
  • Survival Bosses: Face mini-bosses at levels 10, 25, and 50

💰 Progression System

Gold-Based Upgrades: After you die, spend collected gold in the shop to permanently upgrade your stats. This is how you progress and get stronger for future runs!

⚔️ Level-Up System

Each level-up offers choices between different upgrades, weapons, equipment, and attributes. Build your character strategically! The depth of combinations is part of the fun.

🎯 Manual Aim Bonus

Using manual aim (right stick) with high Precision attribute gives you:
• Increased critical hit chance
• Bonus gold from kills
• Aim-assist to help you target enemies
